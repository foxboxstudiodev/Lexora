# Lexora Level Quality Rules

Every Lexora level must pass these rules before release.

## Word rules

- Words must be real.
- Words must belong to the selected language.
- No invented words.
- No random abbreviations.
- No duplicate words after normalization.
- Main words and bonus words must not overlap.
- Every word must be buildable from the level letters.

## Grid rules

- Main words must be placed in the crossword grid.
- The grid must be readable on mobile screens.
- Words should intersect when possible.
- The grid must not be unnecessarily wide or tall.
- Empty cells must not be visible.

## Difficulty rules

- Early levels use 3 or 4 letters.
- Middle levels use 4 to 6 letters.
- Later levels use 6 or 7 letters.
- Difficulty must grow gradually.
- A level must not jump from easy to frustrating.

## Language rules

Supported languages:

- English
- Spanish
- Russian
- Turkish

Each language must use its own validated word list.

## Release rule

A level is release-ready only when it is playable, valid, readable, and not filled with weak dictionary garbage.
