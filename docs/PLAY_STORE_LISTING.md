## Full description

```text
Lexora is a multilingual word-connect crossword puzzle game built for quick, relaxing, and challenging word play.

Swipe through selectable letters, form real words, fill the crossword grid, collect coins, use hints, unlock progress, and explore levels across multiple languages.

Current gameplay includes:

• 13 playable languages
• 300 levels per language
• 3900 total runtime levels
• Repeating 20-level difficulty blocks
• Exact selectable wheel progression from 4 to 10 units
• Clean crossword layouts with real intersections
• Touch-friendly letter wheel controls
• Hints, coins, achievements, daily rewards, and progress saving
• Support for Latin, Cyrillic, Hindi, Chinese, Japanese, and Korean word units

Languages included:

English, Spanish, Russian, Turkish, German, Portuguese, Italian, French, Azerbaijani, Hindi, Chinese, Japanese, and Korean.

Lexora is designed as an original word puzzle game with its own name, code, layout, progression, and content system.
```
